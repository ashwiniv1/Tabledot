Cancellation Policy

Refunds

Disputes for unsatisfactory service are encouraged to be handled between the hosts and cook. If the host is still unsatisfied with the options (if any) provided by the Cook, then Table Dot will attempt to mediate and resolve the concern with the host.

Cancellations by Host

In order to provide you with the freshest ingredients, there is an open window for the cook to purchase your groceries. Any cancellations by the host that occur within the 36 hour appointment window, Table Dot will attempt to contact the cook to request no food purchase/delivery, in the event that the food items have already been purchased, the cook will deliver the food items, but not perform the meal experience. The second component to the total charges is the labor, cancellations for labor are at the discretion of the cook. These policies are selected when cook sets up their profile and can vary from flexible, moderate, and strict. These policies can be viewed on the cook profile for reference.

Cancellations by Cook

Any cancellations will be attempted to be resolved by reaching our next available cook. Please reach out to our customer service department for assistance.
